About

Cybersecurity is the process of protecting systems, networks and programs from digital threats. These attacks aim to access, change, and destroy sensitive information, extort money, or interrupting normal business processes. Unlike IT security, cybersecurity practices from within the computer. IT security practices from only the user's perspective like asking you to fill in your password or other firewalls for security.

Data privacy is important. It is why people put locks on filing cabinets and open safety deposit boxes at banks. As more of our data becomes digitized, we share much more information online than we ever have. Data privacy related to how a piece of information should be handled based on its relevance and importance to the situation.

This generation is often referred to as the “Cloud” generation because everything and everyone is connected through the internet. We are digitizing things that are valuable to us – pictures, money, and private information – but this has not stopped criminals from stealing these valuables. Hackers and scammers are constantly trying to steal people’s sensitive information using different techniques that most people are not aware of. For these people that own electronic devices and store their own valuable personal information, it is imperative that they understand how hacking happens so they can learn how to avoid it.
