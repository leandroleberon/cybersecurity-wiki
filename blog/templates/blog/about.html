About

A cyber attack is a malicious and throughout attempt by an individual or organization to breach the information from a system of another user or organization. Attackers are seeking some kind benefit from the disruptions of the networks. Jasmine Farahani states “data is becoming more and more insecure as companies increasingly use software and cloud storage” (et al. p. 32). The cloud is not as secure as a physical hard drive, so it makes it much easier for attackers to access your personal information as well as company data.
